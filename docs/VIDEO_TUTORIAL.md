## Script Outline

### Scene 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:30)

**Visual**: Logo animation, tagline "Convert Java mods to Bedrock in minutes"

**Voiceover**:
> "Welcome to ModPorter AI! In this quick tutorial, I'll show you how to convert your favorite Minecraft Java Edition mods to Bedrock Edition add-ons using our AI-powered platform. Let's get started!"

---

### Scene 2: Upload Your Mod (0:30 - 1:30)

**Visual**: Screen recording showing:
1. Navigate to modporter.ai
2. Drag and drop a .jar file OR
3. Paste a CurseForge/Modrinth URL

**Voiceover**:
> "First, head to modporter.ai and sign in. You can upload your mod in two ways: either drag and drop your .jar file directly, or paste a link from CurseForge or Modrinth. We accept both individual mods and modpacks!"

**On-screen Text**:
- Supported formats: .jar, .zip
- Max file size: 100MB (Free tier)

---

### Scene 3: Configure Options (1:30 - 2:00)

**Visual**: Show options panel:
- Enable Smart Assumptions
- Include Dependencies toggle

**Voiceover**:
> "Before converting, you can configure a few options. Smart Assumptions helps adapt Java-only features to work in Bedrock. We recommend keeping this enabled for the best results. You can also choose to include mod dependencies automatically."

---

### Scene 4: Conversion Process (2:00 - 3:00)

**Visual**: Real-time progress screen showing:
- Analysis stage (0-25%)
- Mapping stage (25-50%)
- Conversion stage (50-75%)
- Packaging stage (75-100%)

**Voiceover**:
> "Now the magic happens! Our AI analyzes your mod, maps Java features to Bedrock equivalents, translates the code, and packages everything into a ready-to-install .mcaddon file. You can watch the progress in real-time. Most mods take 5-15 minutes."

---

### Scene 5: Download & Install (3:00 - 4:00)

**Visual**: Show completed conversion with:
- Download button
- Installation instructions

**Voiceover**:
> "When conversion is complete, you'll get a notification. Click download to save your .mcaddon file. Double-click it to install in Minecraft Bedrock, or copy it to your Minecraft add-ons folder. That's it!"

---

### Scene 6: Explore Features (4:00 - 4:30)

**Visual**: Quick tour of:
- Dashboard (conversion history)
- Comparison view
- Behavior editor
- Pricing page

**Voiceover**:
> "Want to do more? Check out our Dashboard for conversion history, use Comparison View to analyze differences, or try the Behavior Editor to customize your converted add-on. Visit our Pricing page to see subscription options."

---

### Scene 7: Conclusion (4:30 - 5:00)

**Visual**: Return to homepage with CTA buttons

**Voiceover**:
> "Thanks for watching! Ready to try it yourself? Head to modporter.ai and convert your first mod for free. If you have questions, check our FAQ or join our Discord community. Happy modding!"

**On-screen Text**:
- modporter.ai

## Production Notes

### Equipment Needed
- Screen recording software (OBS Studio recommended)
- Microphone (USB condenser recommended)
- Video editing software (DaVinci Resolve or Adobe Premiere)

### Key Highlights for Editor
1. Keep transitions smooth (0.5s fade)
2. Add subtle background music (royalty-free)
3. Use consistent lower-thirds for on-screen text
4. Include chapter markers for YouTube

### Accessibility Requirements
- Closed captions required
- Audio descriptions available
- High contrast text overlay
