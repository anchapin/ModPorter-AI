## FAQ

### How does billing work?

**Free tier**: No billing required.

**Pay-as-you-go**: Charged per conversion at time of conversion. No monthly commitment.

**Pro tier**: Monthly subscription billed on your billing date. Overage charges applied at month end.

**Enterprise**: Invoiced monthly based on usage. Net 30 terms available.

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only)

### Can I switch plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time:

- **Upgrade**: Immediate effect, prorated billing
- **Downgrade**: Applied at next billing cycle

### What about refunds?

- **Failed conversions**: Full refund if we cannot deliver a usable add-on
- **Unused conversions**: Pro subscribers can roll over up to 5 unused conversions to next month
- **Subscription cancellation**: No refund for current period, access continues until billing date

### Are there any hidden fees?

No. All prices are clearly listed. The only potential extra charges are:

- Overage charges for Pro subscribers exceeding their monthly allocation
- Storage overages beyond your plan limit ($0.10/GB/day)

### Do you offer discounts for open source projects?

Yes. Qualifying open source Minecraft mod projects receive 50% off Pro tier. [Apply here](https://forms.gle/portkit-oss-application).

### What happens if a conversion fails?

If our conversion engine produces a result that fails validation or cannot be loaded in Bedrock Edition, you receive a full credit for that conversion. You can use the credit for a retry or request a refund.
