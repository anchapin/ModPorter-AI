## 🎮 What is ModPorter-AI?

ModPorter-AI is an AI-powered tool that automatically converts your Minecraft Java Edition mods to Bedrock Edition add-ons. No manual coding required – just upload your JAR file and get a ready-to-install .mcaddon!

### Here's what makes it special:

✅ **Smart Mode Detection** – Automatically identifies if your mod is client-only, server-only, or universal

✅ **One-Click Conversion** – Our AI handles all the complex translation

✅ **Batch Processing** – Convert multiple mods at once

✅ **Error Auto-Recovery** – Smart retry logic handles most issues automatically
