**Phase Goal**: Implement comprehensive conversion success metrics tracking and reporting with detailed breakdowns by category, complexity, and error type.

**Requirement**: REQ-4.3

---

## Deliverables

1. **Success Rate Tracking** - Track and calculate conversion success rates overall and by category

4. **Reporting Dashboard** - Display metrics in conversion reports with visualizations
5. **Trend Analysis** - Track success rate trends over time

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Metrics to Track
- Total conversions attempted
- Successful conversions (complete)
- Partial success (needs review)
- Failed conversions (complete failure)
- Average conversion time
- Error types encountered

### Success Rate Categories
- **Overall**: All conversions
- **By Complexity**: Simple/Standard/Complex/Expert
- **By Error Type**: Transient/Permanent/Resource
- **By Time Period**: Daily/Weekly/Monthly

### Success Thresholds
- **Complete Success**: 100% semantic equivalence, no errors
- **Partial Success**: 70%+ semantic equivalence, fixable errors
- **Failed**: <70% semantic equivalence or critical errors
