## Technical Implementation Notes

### Quality Score Components
- **Syntax Validity** (20%): Are generated files syntactically correct?
- **Semantic Equivalence** (30%): How semantically similar is output to input?
- **Behavior Preservation** (30%): Are behaviors preserved in conversion?
- **Completeness** (20%): Was the full mod converted?

### Issue Categories
- **Critical**: Breaks functionality, must fix
- **Major**: Significant impact, should fix
- **Minor**: Cosmetic or minor impact

### Scoring Thresholds
- **Excellent**: 90%+
- **Good**: 70-89%
- **Needs Work**: <70%
